Team Info

The Kids Next Door is a Pro-Am Team in NBA 2k which was created in the March of 2017. The Team colors are purple white and black. There have been 10 total members that have ever played a game as a Kid Next Door. The team orginated from a previous team called Ovo's Finest which was later disbanded and recreated to The Kids Next Door.
