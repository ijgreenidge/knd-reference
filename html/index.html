Team Info

The Kids Next Door Pro-Am Team in Nba 2k was created in March of 2017. The Team colors are purple white and black. There have been 10 total members that have ever played a game as a Kid Next Door. The team orginated from a previous team called Ovo's Finest which was later disband and recreated to what we have now.

Current Team Record: 20-9
