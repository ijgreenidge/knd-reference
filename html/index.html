Team Info

The Kids Next Door is a Pro-Am Team in Nba 2k which was created in March of 2017. The Team colors are purple white and black. There have been 10 total members that have ever played a game as a Kid Next Door. The team orginated from a previous team called Ovo's Finest which was later disband and recreated to what we have now.

Current Team Record: 20-9

We are Elite! We became elite in 28 games many great performances through those 28 games and it was a true team effort. It's time for us to face the Elite division and see our skills develop and our team chemistry become stronger.
